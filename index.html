About Us:

A+ Rated BBB-Accredited business

G&R|Construction was established in 2009.

We started as a General Handyman business in 1996 and have evolved our company to focus on general contracting work. Our professionalism and dedication to our clients and projects has enabled us to grow. Serving the Fresno community for over 40 years has given us experience in all aspects of residential and commerical repairs and improvements. We have experience in carpentry, flooring, countertops, lighting and electrical wiring, painting, roofing and more.
